About Us

Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, was founded with the noble vision to raise professionals and leaders of high academic calibre and unblemished character, nurtured with a strong motivation and commitment to serve humanity. The institution is the fulfillment of the vision of its founders Late Dr. D. G. S. Dhinakaran and the present Chancellor Dr. Paul Dhinakaran. Established in 1986 as an Engineering College, it was granted an autonomous status in 1999 and declared Deemed-to-be University by the University Grants Commission (UGC) in recognition of its academic excellence by the Ministry of Human Resources Development under section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956 vide notification no 9-3-2000-U3 dated 23.6.2004 of the Government of India. Accredited by NAAC, the institution offers a wide array of Bachelors, Masters and Doctoral Degree programmes in various branches of Engineering, Biotechnology, Food processing and Agricultural Sciences. From a humble beginning of 180 students, the institution has rapidly grown to 8491 students and 452 faculty representing 18 academic departments.

Campus Life

Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences has taken smart initiatives towards creating a Green campus in the areas of green computing and waste management. Students are housed in thirteen different, well-furnished hostels with all the necessary facilities provided within the campus. Drone Technology

Farming is the first and prime occupation of human beings yet has never become obsolete. Prevalence of water scarcity and non-availability of scientific techniques has made this occupation one of the most neglected in India. With the advent in Industrial revolution (Industry 4.0) and Information Communication Technology, the agriculture sector has witnessed a major come-back. The need for usage of modern technologies like Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Block Chain, Machine Learning, Robotics and Automation for converting the Conventional Agriculture to Digital Agriculture is being explored and implemented. The mission envisages development of IoT & AI based Smart Farming Systems for monitoring the climatic adaptations, soil health, plant health, pest and disease infestations using sensors and finally create a holistic knowledge ecosystem through training, Research & Development and outreach & consultancy.
